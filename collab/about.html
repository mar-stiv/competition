Make the best you can to save our planet



Nowadays we are more aware about global warming but does everyone know the consequences?

In the latest years, the number of people that have health problems are increasing. Air pollution can cause lung problems, for example; asthma attacks, bronchial diseases and allergies. Unfortunately, that is not all, air pollution can also cause skin cancers and even vision problems. Sea pollution is also a huge deal when it comes to helping our environment. A lot of plastic and rubbish is being thrown into the sea which is causing a serious threat to the aquatic life.

What are we doing to make a change?

When you by products on our website, on our end we try to use the least amount of plastic. Instead we use cardboard packaging to reduce sea pollution. We also try to use the most environmentally friendly transport depending on your location.
